# Task 4 - Data Visualization

## Objective

Analyze sales data and create visualizations to identify trends, patterns, and business insights.

## Tools Used

* Microsoft Excel
* Charts and Graphs

## Dataset

Sales dataset containing 1200 records with product, customer, payment, and order information.

## Visualizations Created

* Product-wise Total Sales Analysis
* Order Status Distribution
* Sales by Payment Method
* Top 5 Customers by Spending
* Referral Source Performance Analysis
* Average Product Price Analysis
* Product-wise Quantity Sold Analysis

## Key Insights

* Chair generated the highest total sales.
* Chair sold the highest quantity of units.
* Credit Card generated the highest sales among payment methods.
* Instagram was the top-performing referral source.
* Customer C38840 was the highest-spending customer.
* Phones had the highest average product price.

## Outcome

Created interactive and informative charts that transformed raw sales data into meaningful business insights, enabling better understanding of customer behavior, product performance, and sales trends.
